WEB/UI DESIGN by LUKE

websites should be seen and heard and felt and tasted.

danceoftheplanets.com

a standalone website & solar system sonification for the Los Angeles band Desert Magic.

I strongly believe that if you're a band, your website should not only give information about you, but also be a music tool with some use-value in the lives of your fans. I still run into people that regularly use this website as a yoga/meditation soundtrack.

The world needs more band websites like this.

sixusefulbooks.com

A starkly minimal website for a small press that I run with my partner Blair.

giantframe.com

A website I made for my music-for-film business. I wanted it to feel homebrewed and, for lack of a better word, boutique. I think it has a weird and cool aesthetic that I don't see very much on the internet.

this site

You're already here! Take a look around! I designed this site to be a portfolio and a calling card. I want it to immediately communicate who I am and what I do. It's a work in progress.

further explorations

Beyond the sites I've actually published, I've done a lot of experimentaiton with 3D websites, sound-making websites, and video-game-like websites. If you want a website that is interactive, strange, or ornamental, I'd love to envision something with you.
