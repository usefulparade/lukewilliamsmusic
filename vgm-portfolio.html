GAMES I'VE MADE

ONWARD is a music-controlled game that I built for Redcat Studio Spring 2017. I loved the challenge of trying to build systems that really felt like they were listening & responding to your real-world soundmaking. The game was designed to be performed collaboratively by a group of musicians and one traditional player, which forced us to consider the stage narratives of button-pressing.

I'm still working on building this concept out into something more generalizable, but in the meantime a playable demo is available to all my patrons.

Back when organ grinders played movie scores live, the music held a certain power and weight over the experience of the watcher. How can we give that experience to the player?

A NIGHT DEITY June 30, 2016

A NIGHT deity is a music tool that makes polyrhythms & polygonal constellations.

Made in just a few days for the #mysticwestern game jam.

If you find the secret mode & screenshot it, I'll name a constellation after you.

SIGNAL RECORDING AND ANALYSIS INTERNSHIP September 2016
