Hi! I'm Luke ^_^

I'm a musician making slow motion jaw-looseners, soundtracks for podcasts and spacewalks, and a bunch of other stuff. I founded the filmscore collective giant frame, and my daily music-for-gifs project just wrapped up its second season.

I'm also a creative coder and game-maker, interested in small art and music tools. You might like this solar system sonification or this skating game that I'm currently developing with my brother.

I send monthly bundles of paintings, music, code, and writing to a bunch of people on patreon, and my partner and I run the design co-op Useful Parade.

If you want the project firehose, dig through the archive. I post drawings on instagram, and I occasionally tweet about games and geology.
