Hi! I'm Luke ^_^

I'm a musician making slow motion jaw-looseners and songs about space and trains and feelings. I'm a composer and sound designer on the podcast reply all and I've done a lot of other scoring stuff, including two seasons of a daily music-for-gifs project.

I'm also a creative coder and game developer, curious about small tools for art and music. You might like this solar system sonification, or this skating game that my brother and I just released!

I send monthly bundles of drawings, music, writing to some lovely people on patreon, and my partner and I run a design co-op called Useful Parade.

If you want the project firehose, dig through the archive. I post drawings on instagram, and I occasionally tweet about games and geology.
